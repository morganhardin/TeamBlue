Scenario 4:

Arthur, Family Dinner

Arthur and his family were planning on getting together Friday night after work in order to catch up with one another. They typically go to someoneâ€™s house, but this time decided to go out to eat. Arthur knows that he has a big family, so he decided they should schedule a reservation for the restaurant they were going to since they would be going during the busy dining hours on a Friday night. He needs to make reservations for a minimum of 12 people and also knows how much of a hassle it can be trying to make a reservation for a big party.


Arthur created a group chat with his family to confirm who all was going to be attending the get together on Friday. He confirmed that 15 members of his family were coming, including 3 children. He is then able to go onto the SmartReserve app on his smart phone and book a reservation for 12 adults and 3 children, a total of 15 people, for Friday at 6:00.


As it gets closer to Friday, Arthur gets a text from his cousin saying that he can now attend the get together Friday night at the restaurant. Arthur acknowledges that it is a last minute change to the reservation and that he cannot promise if he can get it changed. Arthur goes back onto the SmartReserve app and clicks edit reservation. He is able to see that the only changes he can make is to lower the total number of people. He cannot add anyone to his reservation because it says the restaurant is completely booked for that day and time. Arthur is able to tell his cousin that the restaurant was booked and he could not change the reservation. On Friday at 6:00, Arthur and his family walk into the restaurant. Arthur goes to the host station and says he has a reservation at 6:00 and tells them his name. They are seated immediately since the restaurant was able to see the reservation for 15 people and prepared in advance for their arrival.

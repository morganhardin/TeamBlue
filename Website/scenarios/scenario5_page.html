Scenario 5:

Arthur, Date Night

Arthur was planning on surprising his girlfriend with a date night to a high-end restaurant. He was planning on making a reservation for 4:00 on the following Saturday but has to wait until the last minute to find out if his girlfriend works or not. Four days before that Saturday, Arthur decided to just go ahead and book a reservation for 2 people, even though he still was not sure if his girlfriend had to work.


The next day, Arthur’s girlfriend tells him she doesn’t have to work so she is able to go on Saturday. Arthur goes back into the SmartReserve app to edit the reservation to be by a window since he thought that was more romantic. He saves the changes to his reservation and continues through the week.


The day before, Arthur gets a text from his girlfriend saying someone called in sick and would be out for the next few days. That meant that she would have to work that Saturday and would no longer be available to go out to eat. Arthur understands and goes back into SmartReserve for a third time to cancel their reservation. Before he can completely cancel it, he decides to just change the reservation for the same date, time, and window seat but for the next Saturday. He saves the changes and the two are able to go to dinner the following week.

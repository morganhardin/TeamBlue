Scenario 17:

John, Canceling Dinner Plans

John was planning on taking his wife and two children out to eat since his kids were about to start college and he wanted to spend time with them before they left. John had a hard time picking between his two children’s favorite restaurants, so he decided to choose a place that everyone would like. Once he made a choice, he decided to go ahead and make a reservation that way they did not have to worry about waiting to be seated.


John started a new reservation and decided to make it for Tuesday night at 6:30. He knew no one had to work at that time and his children were busy over the weekend so that was the only day available to go out to eat. John made sure he marked a party of 4 and he had no preference on where to be seated. He just wanted to have a nice dinner with his family before his kids went off to college.


The morning of the reservation, John realizes that he is going to have to work late. There had been a major problem over the weekend that they were working on fixing so his work had gotten backed up. John felt horrible since he would no longer get to go out to eat with his family that night. Luckily, his son got called into work so he could not go anymore. John goes into the SmartReserve application on his phone and sees his reservation for 6:30 that night. He clicks “Edit Reservation” and is then able to scroll to the bottom and click “Cancel Reservation”. After he presses that, another screen pops up that asks if he was sure he wanted to cancel his reservation. John clicks yes to cancel his reservation and apologizes to his family and promises to take them out another night.

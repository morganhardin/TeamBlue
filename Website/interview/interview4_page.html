Interview 4: Retired Woman

Conducted By: Morgan Hardin on October 15, 2023


Who are you?

I am retired at 72 and I used to work for the highway department. I usually have my grandkids with me and I love spending time with them. I am not very up-to-date on technology and usually get my granddaughter’s help with my phone and iPad.


Have you ever worked in a restaurant?

No, I have never worked in a restaurant and I would never want to either.


As a customer, what is the most frustrating thing about being a customer in a restaurant while waiting to be seated?

The most frustrating thing for me is not knowing when you are going to be seated. They always give you a time frame and it’s never right.


Does it matter to you if you are seated by a window or in a booth or table?

Yes, I always prefer a booth over a table. A window would also be nice as well.


Would you prefer to call a restaurant to make a reservation or go through an app or website?

I would prefer to call and make a reservation at a restaurant but I usually don’t make reservations.


Is there anything frustrating or confusing about making a reservation through a restaurant? If so, what would make it easier to use?

I think it is very frustrating when you have to be put on hold multiple times just to reach the right person when calling to make a reservation. It would be easier to have a specific phone number to call to make them or to have the person answering the main phone to schedule the reservations.

Welcome to SmartReserve

The application for high-end restaurants to easily schedule and track reservations.

Get Started

What We Do

Easy-to-use application for reservation scheduling and tracking.

Scheduling

Offers the ability to schedule, cancel, and edit reservations for a specific date and time

Tracking

Tracks reservation date and times and the tables currently available or not

Calendar

View that allows employees to see all reservation and times for any date

Features

Our tracking system is simple to learn and easy to manage that allows employees to keep track of tables that are available or not.

  • Shows the times for the day and with the reservations marked as busy under their respective times.
  • Keeps track of total occupancy and the number of seats left available.

Free

SmartReserve is completely free for all customers, beating leading competitors.

Competition

We offer quality software with no cost and a simple to use interface, both for customers and employees.

Services

SmartReserve makes consumer lives easier with a simple and cost-friendly application.

Customer Mode

This mode only offers scheduling, canceling, and editing the reservation the customer creates.

Employee Mode

This mode has the same functionality as the customer mode, but also has an authorized section that allows access to all features.
